About

In pursuit of global Sustainable Development Goals, understanding and mitigating climate change impacts is paramount. Among these, air temperature emerges as a critical factor with far-reaching consequences. It affects agriculture, ecosystems, energy systems, and even industries. This project provides a real-time tool to forecast all climate-related data for Egypt using advanced machine learning models. The project utilizes Linear Regression and SARIMAX models to capture seasonal patterns from historical data (1990-2023) and provide accurate forecasts. These models are evaluated using the Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE) metric. The ultimate goal is to enhance climate prediction accuracy, aiding in better planning and decision-making for infrastructure, insurance, energy policies, and business development, thereby contributing to sustainable technology and climate resilience efforts.
